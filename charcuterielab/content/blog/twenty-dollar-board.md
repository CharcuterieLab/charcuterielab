title: "The $20 Board That Looks Like $80"
date: "2026-04-23"
image: "/images/blog-budget-board.jpg"
excerpt: "A budget board works when every ingredient has a job and the styling does more of the lifting."
---

The trick is not buying more. It is choosing ingredients that create contrast: one salty meat, one creamy cheese, one bright fruit, one crunchy item, and one small sweet accent.

Use folds, piles, and repetition to make the board feel abundant. A single pack of prosciutto can look generous when every slice is shaped with intention.

Finish with one fresh element, like herbs or grapes, to give the whole board lift.
