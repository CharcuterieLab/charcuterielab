Blue cheese can feel like the loudest ingredient on the board, but honey gives it a structure that makes the whole bite click. The sugar softens the sharp edges, while the cheese gives the honey something savory to cling to.

For an easy board moment, place a wedge of blue cheese near toasted walnuts, crisp apple slices, and a small pot of honey. Let guests build one bite with all four elements.

The rule of thumb: the saltier and funkier the cheese, the darker and more floral the honey can be.
