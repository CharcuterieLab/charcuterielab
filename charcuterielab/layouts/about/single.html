Our Mission

Charcuterie Education for Home Entertainers

Charcuterie Lab exists to answer one question most food content never bothers to ask: why does this work?

Why does blue cheese and honey trigger a dopamine response? Why does the region where prosciutto is made count as an ingredient? Why does cold cheese taste like nothing?

The pairing science behind great charcuterie is genuinely fascinating — and almost nobody talks about it. We talk about almost nothing else.
